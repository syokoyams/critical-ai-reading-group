On February 6th, 2025, we held our inaugural reading group session to discuss the conclusion chapter of Matteo Pasquinelli's *The Eye of the Master* (2023), exploring the relationship between artificial intelligence, labour, and capitalism.

![Reading group session on February 6th, 2025](/assets/2025-02-06-flyer.png)

![Reading group group photo on February 6th, 2025](/assets/2025-02-06-group-photo.jpeg)

Thank you, everyone, for joining our first gathering! Keen observational analyses through and through on how AI is likely to affect white-collar jobs. Not to mention its known impact on the global working class (what Mary Gray and Siddharth Suri call "[ghost work](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghost_work)"). Looking forward to many more sessions to come, so that AI initiatives on our campus remain cognizant of the longer history of labor relations.
